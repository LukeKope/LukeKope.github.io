RENOV8 Logo Design

RENOV8 is an augmented reality interior design company I fabricated that allows for you to preview how furniture or different paint colors would look in your home prior to purchasing them. I was tasked to make a logo for this fictional company and you can see my process below.

First iteration

For this step, I made eight designs in various distinct styles. At this stage, I was still unsure as to what aesthetic I wanted the company to have. Therefore, I tried to make each design have its own distinct style. After receiving feedback on this stage, I took the three designs I thought to be most promising and iterated on them.

Second iterations

For this phase, I took three of the designs from stage two and iterated upon each one. I described my thought process for each art board. The design I felt was most promising after iterating was the couch design at the bottom. I did not disregard the iterations of the other two designs, however. I incorporated aspects of what worked for each design into the final after getting feedback and reviewing my work.

Couch logo iterations

This couch design was the one I felt to be the most promising, therefore I completed the most iterations on it. Going into the next phase, I realized after some feedback that my logos had too much detail for a logo. There were many clashing colors and ideas, so my task was to strip the design down to the essentials to make the logo really pop.
